## Problem

Dependencies are not pinned to exact versions, risking unexpected breakage from upstream updates.

## Fix

Add `flask-wtf>=1.2.1` and `flask-talisman>=1.1.0`. Generate a pinned `requirements.lock` via `pip freeze`. Add `safety check` to CI workflow.
